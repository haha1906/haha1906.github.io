Do not spread false rumors. Don't don't cooperate with evil people by giving false testimony.
Don't participate in wrongdoing, even when the majority is involved in it. Don't trample justice by following the crowd during a lawsuit. At a trial, don't even take the side of a poor person just because he is poor. And don't trample someone's rights just because he is poor.
If you come across an ox or donkey that your enemy has lost, take it back to its owner.
If you see your enemy's donkey fall down under its load, don't leave it that way. Help that man to get his donkey on its feet.
Don't participate in false accusations, and do not let yourself be involved in condemning innocent people to death. I will not leave such people without punishment.
Do not take bribes, for a bribe makes you ignore something that you clearly see. A bribe makes even a righteous person twist the truth.
Don't ill-treat foreigners. You know very well what it is like to be a foreigner. You yourselves were once foreigners in Miṣr. Lev 24:22 In the court, apply laws in the same way to both yourselves and to foreigners.
Deu 17:6 If you give someone the death penalty, there must be at least two or three witnesses. Do not take action with the words of just one witness. Deu 17:7 The witnesses should throw stones first, and then all the community should stone him. This way, you will get rid of the evil.
Plant and harvest your crops for six years. But let the land rest on the seventh year. Do not work on the land during the year of rest, but let poor people use whatever grows on its own. Wild animals can have what is left. Do the same with your vineyards and olive gardens.
Work six days a week, and rest on the seventh day. Let your bull and donkey also rest, as well as your slaves and foreigners who have come to your house.
My angel will lead you to the land of the Amūribbūn, al-Ḥittiyyūn, Farizziyyūn, Kanʿāniyyūn, Ḥawiyyūn and Yabūsiyyūn. When you are possessing the land, don't worship or serve their gods, and don't perform their customs. On the contrary, completely destroy their idols, and break down the stones they set up. Because you belong to Me! If you serve Me, I will add blessings to your food and water, and I will keep you from all kinds of diseases.
