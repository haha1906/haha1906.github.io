On the way "We gave scripture, wisdom, and prophethood to the Children of Isrāʾīl; We provided them with good things and favoured them above others." (Al-Jāthiya 45:16)
So, the people of Allāh (s.w.t.) started out towards the promised land. The shortest way to Kanʿān was through the land of the Qawm al-Jālūt. But Allāh (s.w.t.) did not lead them through that road. He said: "If they come face to face to war, they will desire to go back to Miṣr", and led the people through the desert road towards Baḥr al-Qilzūm. a
Nabī Mūsā also took the bones of nabī Yūsuf with him. This was because Yūsuf (a.s.) made the children of Isrāʾīl swear before his death when he said, "Allāh (s.w.t.) certainly will help you. He will bring you out of Miṣr. When that happens, take my bones with you." Banī Isrāʾīl left the town Sukkūt and came to Itām which is on the edge of the desert. Day and night Allāh (s.w.t.) was with His people. Allāh's jalāl was shining upon them, during the daytime in the form of pillar of cloud, and during the nighttime as a pillar of fire.
