The meeting with Allāh "Once again, We gave Mūsā the Scripture, perfecting [Our favour] for those who do good, explaining everything clearly, as guidance and mercy, so that they (Banī Isrāʾīl) might believe in the meeting with their Lord." (Al-Anʿām 6:154)
At that point Allāh (a.j.) gave these words to Mūsā (a.s.) a for Banī Isrāʾīl:
"I am al-Qayyūm, your Provider who brought you out of Miṣr, where you were slaves. Do not worship other ilāhayn except Me. Do not make idols for yourselves that look like anything located in heaven, on earth or in the water. Do not bow down before them or serve them. Because I am an Ilāh of jealosy, the sin of those who hate Me will affect your children up to the third or fourth generation, but I will never spare My love from those who love Me and obey My commands.
Do not misuse My name. I am your Rabb. A man who uses My name out of place will not be left unpunished.b
Keep the day of rest holy.
Respect your father and mother, so that your life will be long in the land I am giving to you.
Do not commit murder.
Do not commit adultery.
Do not steal.
Do not give false witness.
Do not desire to possess another person's house, his wife, his slaves, his ox, his donkey, or anything else that he owns.
Banī Isrāʾīl did not want to be muqarrab to Allāh
The people were very afraid. The voice of the thunder and the trumpet, and the light from all the flashes of lightning had put big fear in their hearts. They begged the messenger of Allāh: "Please, speak to us yourself and we will agree to whatever you say, but do not let Allāh (s.w.t.) speak, otherwise we will die." They did not want to go up to the mountain and be connected to Allāh.c "Do not be afraid," said prophet Mūsā. "Why are you afraid? It is just a test. Allāh (s.w.t.) made these miracles appear to you in order to make you turn back from sin." But the people did not want to come close, so they stayed a long way off.
19:20 Then Allāh (s.w.t.) called Mūsā (a.s.) to the mountain and said: 19:21 "Go, tell the people that from now on they should not put their feet on the mountain. Whoever passes the border in order to see, will perish. 19:22 From now on I will appoint priests from among yourselves. \it But even they must purify themselves before approaching Me. 19:24 Go down, and bring Hārūn to the mountain."
