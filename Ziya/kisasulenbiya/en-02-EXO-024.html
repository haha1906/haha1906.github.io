Banī Isrāʾīl accepts the covenant of Sharīʿa
Then Allāh (s.w.t.) commanded prophet Mūsā and Hārūn to go down and bring up Nadab ibn Hārūn, Abihu ibn Hārūn and 70 peoplea from among the elders of Isrāʾīl to the mountain. But He forbade Banī Isrāʾīl to go up the mountain. Mūsā (a.s.) went and told the people all the instructions given by Allāh (s.w.t.). Everybody said together, "We will do everything the Creator has commanded." Then nabī Mūsā put all the commandments Allāh (s.w.t.) had given into writing.
The next morning he built an altar at the foot of the mountain. There they slaughtered sacrifices for Allāh (s.w.t.) and put the blood into basins. Then they read the Covenant Book to the people. The people all promised, "We will listen to all of Allāh's commands and do what He has said." After that Hazrat Mūsā took the blood in the basin and announced, "Allāh is making a covenant with you. This blood will be the witness for the words I have just read to you."b
Under the Glory of Allāh (s.w.t.)
Then Hazrat Mūsā, Hārūn, Nadāb, Abīhū and the seventy elders from Isrāʾīl went up the mountain to the place Allāh (s.w.t.) had said.
Above them there was a stage like a sapphire stone and it was clear like sky, and Allāh's glory was glowing above it. Allāh did not harm the elders of Banī Isrāʾīl. They dined the feast under the stage and ate from the covenant food.
After the feast, Allāh (s.w.t.) told the prophet Mūsā to come to His presence. Allāh (s.w.t.) was going to give him stone tablets on which the laws were written. "I will go up, to the presence of Allāh (s.w.t.)," said Mūsā (a.s.) to the others. "You wait for me here. If there are any disputes while I am gone, act according to the advice of Hārūn or Ḥūr".c
