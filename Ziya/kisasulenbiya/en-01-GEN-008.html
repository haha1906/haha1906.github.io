The Covenant made with Nūḥ (a.s.) "Nūḥ, descend in peace from Us, with blessings on you" (Hūd 11:48).
When the flood waters went away and the ground became dry again, Hazrat Nūḥ came out of the boat with his family and sacrificed one of each kind of halal animal and bird to Allāh (s.w.t.). At that time Allāh (s.w.t.) revealed: "From youth, Bānī Ādam will always struggle against the whispering (waswasa) of the nafs. Even so, I will never curse the surface of the earth like this again — I will never destroy all living creatures. As long as the world exists, there will always be planting and harvesting, hot and cold, summer and winter, day and night".
