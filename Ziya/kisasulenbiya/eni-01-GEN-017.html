The sign of the Covenant made with Hazrat Ibrāhīm
About 13 years of time passed after that. One day Allāh (huwa ʿalā kull shayʾin qadīr) gave a revelation to Ibrāhīm (a.s.) again and said: "Ibrāhīm, be loyal (wafāʿ) to our covenant, value our friendship, and live with taqwā. I will always be with you and protect you. I will increase your descendants very much." Ibrāhīm (a.s.) did sajda. Allāh said, "You will be the father of many nationalities.a I will create many children and nations out of you. Kings will be born from among your descendants. My covenant will continue with you and with your descendants from generation to generation. This earth/land, where you are a visitor/stranger now, I will give to you and to your descendants as everlasting mulk (possession). You (plural, i.e. your descendants) will be under My protection."
"Just keep My covenant: you and also your children be faithful to My covenant always. Every male among you must have his foreskin circumcised. This will be the sign of the covenant between us. Circumcise the male children when they are eight days old. You will also circumcise your slaves, whether you buy them from foreign nations or whether they are born among you. This sign on your bodies is the proof/argument that the covenant I made with you will last forever. If anybody among you does not want to be circumcised, you must remove them from your community, because by doing this he has rejected My covenant."
Isḥāq means "he laughs" "We gave Ibrāhīm the good news of Isḥāq– a prophet and a righteous saliḥ man– and blessed him and Isḥāq too." (Al-Ṣaffāt 37:112-113).
Allāh (s.w.t.) continued speaking and said, "You will not call your wife Sarāyb anymore. From now on her name is Sārah. I will bless her and surely give you a son by her. She will be the mother of many nations. Kings will be born from among her descendants." (The word "Sārah" means "queen".)
Ibrāhīm did sajda. And these thoughts went through his heart: 'I am 100 years old. And Sārah is 90 years old.' He laughed to himself, and he thought: "Will Sārah really bear a child now at this age?" Then he said: "Oh Allāh, please bless my son Ismāʿīl!" 21 Allāh said, "Ibrāhīm! Next year Sārah will bear you a son. You will name him Isḥāq.c I will continue the covenant I made with you, through this son of yours."
"Have peaceful mind about Ismāʿīl", Allāh said afterwards. "I will surely bless him. I will increase his descendants and I will create a big nation out of him. He will be the father of twelve kings."
On that very day, Hazrat Ibrāhīm obeyed Allāh's command: he circumcised all men and male children who were born in his house or whom he had bought. At that time Ibrāhīm (a.s.) was 99 years old, and Ismāʿīl (a.s.) was thirteen. In this way, Hazrat Ibrāhīm, Ismāʿīl (a.s.), and their servants--all males born in his house or bought--were circumcised on that very day.d
