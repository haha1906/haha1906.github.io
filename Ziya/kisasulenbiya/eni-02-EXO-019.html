The covenant made with Banī Isrāʾīla
The daʿwa to the covenant of Ibrāhīm
When it was exactly 2 months after they left Miṣr, they arrived at the desert of Saynāʾ, to the mountain that was in the valley of Tuva. They built a camp there at the foot of the mountain. Mūsā (a.s.) went up to the mountain in order to get guidance from Allāh. Then a voice came to him saying: "Here, I have brought you out of the Miṣr safe and sound, from the land of slavery. From now on you belong to Me. If you listen to My words, and stay faithful to My covenant made with Ibrāhīm, you will be My priceless treasure among all nations. Yes, the whole earth is Mine. But you (plural) will be My holy kingdom, and you will share ʿilm and hidāya to all (other) nations.b Go and deliver My words to the people." c Mūsā (a.s.) came down from the mountain and called the elders and declared to them about the daʿwa of Allāh. Everybody agreed together and promised, "We will do everything Allāh (s.w.t.) has said." \it In this way, Banī Isrāʾīl promised to put their faith in the friendship covenant Allāh (s.w.t.) had made with Hazrat Ibrāhīm (a.s.).
Allāh shows miracle to the nation
Then Allāh (s.w.t.) said to Mūsā (a.s.): "Tell the people that they should purify themselves today and tomorow and wash their clothes. They should be ready on the third day. On the third day I will demonstrate My miracles on Mount Saynāʾ in the sight of all the people. Mark a boundary around the mountain. Then instruct everybody: nobody should come close to the mountain. If anyone sets his foot on the mountain, kill him with stones or arrows. No human hand should touch him. Whether it is an animal or human being, do not let it live. The people should only go up the mountain after a long blast from the ram's horn."
The messenger of Allāh came down from the mountain and said to the people: "Wash your clothes, and be purified. Don't have relations with your wife. Get prepared for the third day!"
On the morning of the third day, there was thunder and lightning on the mountain. A thick cloud covered the mountain, and a very loud trumpet blast was heard everywhere. All the people in the camp were trembling from fear.
Mūsā (a.s.) brought the people out of the camp and took them to the foot of the mountain in order to make them meet Allāh. Then the mountain shook violently. Allāh (s.w.t.) had brought down fire on the mountain, and thick smoke was climbing up to the sky. The voice of the trumpet was growing louder and louder.
