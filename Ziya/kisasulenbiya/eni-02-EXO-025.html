The Hariym of Banī Isrāʾīl during Hijra
The Tent of Munājā
Allāh (s.w.t.) gave instructions to Mūsā (a.s.) on the mountain, in the cloud about the Holy Tent. Banī Isrāʾīl was to do the ibādāt performed as jamāʿa in this Tent during Hijra. And Mūsā (a.s.) was also to meet with Allāh in this Tent:
"Tell Banī Isrāʾīl, may they give their part in the building of Munājā Tent. Do not force anybody saying 'give so much'. If someone gives, it must be from true heart. Here are the things to be given: gold, silver and bronze things, threads/ropes of blue, purple and red, delicate linen, goat's hair, the skins which is ashlangan, acacia wood, olive oil for the lights, fragrant seeds for mushk-anbar and expensive stones. You will use these things for the building of Ḥarīm.aThere will be My Sakīna in the Ḥarīm. When building the Tent, its shape and furniture you will make exactly as I will have told.
The chest of Covenant
Get a box made of acacia wood, two and a half dhiraa' long, 1.5 dhirāʾ wide and 1.5 dhirāʾ tall. Have (them) cover it inside and out with pure gold. Then 4 rings made from gold and fasten them to the four legs of the box from two sides. Then make 2 poles from wood and cover it with gold, and put them through the rings on the two sides of the box. When you (plural) lift the box up, you will hold these poles. Put the stone tablets I will give to you (later), inside the box. On the top of the box, make a lid from pure gold. Its length be 2.5 dhirāʾ, and width - 1.5 dhirāʾ. And you (plural) will make the timthāls of two Cherubims from gold on top of the lid. My Sakīna will descend among the cherubim and I will reveal My commandments to you."
