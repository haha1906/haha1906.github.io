Then Allāh (s.w.t.) spoke to nabī Mūsā: "Now I will send another plague over the Miṣriyyūn, and then he will let you go. He himself will drive all of you out of Miṣr." The messenger of Allāh said to Firʿawn: "Allāh (s.w.t.) says this: "In the dark of the night I will punish Miṣr. Every first-born son in Miṣr — from the son of Firʿawn to the sons of female-slaves and even the first-born of animals — all will die. There will be a mourning in Miṣr which has never been seen before and never will be again. But no harm will be done to Banī Isrāʾīl. From this you will know that I have put a distinction between My people and your people. All of your officials will come to me and bow down before me and ask us to leave Miṣr. We will go after that." Then he left Firʿawn's presence hot with anger.
