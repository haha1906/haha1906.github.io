The walls of the Tent
Let the Munājā Tent be in a 4 corner shape. You will divide the inside of it into two rooms. Use 10 curtains made form delicate linen knit together with blue, purple and red threads. And put the images of cherubims on them. Every curtain must be 28 elbow tall, 4 elbow wide. Knit the long sides of the 5 curtains together separate and another 5 curtains separate. Then you will have two long curtains. To the two sides of these two curtains, knit the halqas made from the blue cloth. Then you will fasten these two curtains with the help of these halqas by pulling with the gold ilgaks.
