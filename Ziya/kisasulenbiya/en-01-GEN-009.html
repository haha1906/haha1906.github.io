Then Allāh blessed Nūḥ (a.s.) and his sons and said: "Be fruitful. Multiply and fill the earth! But from now on, every animal will fear you--whether big or small, whether in the sky or in the sea. Rule over them and take them as your food. Just as I gave the green plants for you to eat, now I give you the animals as well. Just do not eat any animal with its blood still in it, since there is life in the blood. I forbid you to kill people. If anybody sheds the blood of a man, he will owe me a ḥisāb.a This is because I created man as an imitation of my character.b So give the death penalty to both humans and animals that have killed human beings.
I make this covenant with you and with your descendants and with all the living creatures that came out of the boat — the birds and the animals. I will never destroy all the living creatures on the earth with a flood of water. Such a great flood will never happen again."
Allāh (s.w.t.) continued speaking and said: "I will put My rainbow among the clouds. And this rainbow is the sign of My covenant which I have made with you 15 and with all the living creatures. Whenever clouds gather in the sky and My rainbow appears among the clouds, remember the covenant that I made with you. Such a flood that destroys life completely will never be repeated again. Whenever the rainbow appears in the clouds, this will be a sign (aya) of the permanent covenant that I made with all the creatures living on the earth.
When you see a rainbow in the sky, don't forget: Allāh (swt) once destroyed the earth because of mankind's violence and oppression. Be patient for Allāh's deliverance. Don't be careless about your life, like the people of Nūḥ (a.s.).
Nūḥ (a.s.) lived 350c more years after the flood and died at the age of 950.
