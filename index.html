Aalden, My Village

Posted on 25.05.2019

For 2 months I have been living in Aalden. And now I wanna share my experince with you posting this blog. You will see its map below. They are very close to Oosterhesselen also.

Aalden is located in Derente province, as a village of Coevorden gementee

Population of this village 1850, but strange thing is it is like connected to Zweeloo village. It is comparably bigger than other villages near.

Aalden houses are quite cosy and warm.

Like most of the big villages in Nederlands, there is a wind mill in the town center. It works at least once a week, even though there is no wheat harvested.

The cute mollen of Aalden is a good landmark

Nice houses in Aalden calling you "Come Here!"

Do you want to visit here? Come with bus 22 or 27 from Hoogeveen or Emmen.
